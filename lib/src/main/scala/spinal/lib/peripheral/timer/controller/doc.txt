==== IP-Core addresses =====

0x10: read
  0-31: [R ]
    Read lower 32 Bit of mtime

0x14: read
  0-31: [R ]
    Read upper 32 Bit of mtime

0x18: read/write
  0-31: [RW]
    Lower 32 Bit of mtimecmp.
    -> A write to this address will clear the interrupt.

0x1c: read/write
  0-31: [RW]
    Lower 32 Bit of mtimecmp.
    -> A write to this address will clear the interrupt.
